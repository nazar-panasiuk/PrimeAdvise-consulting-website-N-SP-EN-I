Cookie Policy

1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how this website uses cookies and related technologies. The platform focuses exclusively on consulting services, providing guidance, strategic analysis, and business project evaluation. Cookies help ensure a smooth, secure, and optimized user experience.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you browse websites. They support essential functionality, save preferences, and allow anonymized analysis that helps improve consulting content, performance, and usability.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are required for the core operation of the website. They ensure that navigation, content display, and technical processes function properly without interruption.

3.2 Functional Cookies

Functional cookies store user preferences (such as language or interface settings) to provide a more efficient and comfortable browsing experience.

3.3 Analytical Cookies

Analytical cookies help us understand how visitors interact with the site. They provide anonymized insights that enable us to improve consulting guides, project explanations, and strategic content.

4. Purpose of Processing

  • Ensuring the technical stability and proper functioning of the website
  • Saving preferences to enhance user experience
  • Collecting anonymized analytics to optimize consulting materials and strategic content
  • Providing and personalizing consulting-related information

5. Use of Analytical Insights

We use aggregated, anonymized information to assess which consulting articles, business project guides, and technological evaluations are most helpful for visitors. These insights serve exclusively to improve content and learning value.

6. Managing Your Cookie Settings

You may configure your browser to block or delete cookies at any time. Please note that disabling certain cookies may limit the functionality or display of some parts of the website. Browser help sections provide instructions on managing cookie preferences.

7. Third-Party Services

Some features on the site may rely on third-party providers (e.g., analytics or media integrations). We do not control their cookie practices and recommend reviewing their individual privacy policies.

8. Retention Period

Cookies may be stored for varying durations depending on their purpose (session-based or extended-duration). Analytical data is retained in anonymized form and periodically deleted when no longer required for improving consulting content.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect technical adjustments, legal changes, or improvements to our consulting platform. Updated versions will be posted on this page.

10. Contact

If you have questions about Cookies or our data practices, please contact us using the details available on the Contact page.

Note: This website is dedicated to consulting services. All materials are for informational and educational purposes only and do not promote or request the purchase of any restricted financial instruments.

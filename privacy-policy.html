Privacy Policy for Our Consulting Platform

Last Updated: 22.01.2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information while you use our consulting website. We prioritize your privacy and aim to ensure that you understand how your data is processed within the context of consulting services, business guidance, and strategic support.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following types of information:

  • Personal Data: This includes your name, email address, phone number, or other contact details submitted when you sign up for newsletters or contact us through forms.
  • Usage Information: Data about how you interact with the website, such as IP address, browser type, visit duration, and pages viewed.
  • Feedback and Comments: Information you provide through comments, inquiries, or feedback related to consulting topics or business guidance.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect may be used for the following purposes:

  • Providing Consulting Content: Ensuring proper website functionality and delivering consulting guides, strategic analyses, and business project explanations.
  • Improving Content: Anonymized usage insights help us enhance our consulting materials and create more relevant content.
  • Communication: Using your contact information to send updates, newsletters, or notifications about new consulting materials.
  • Legal Requirements: Complying with applicable laws and regulations.

3. Disclosure of Information

We do not sell or rent your data. However, we may share information in the following cases:

  • Service Providers: External partners who support website operation, analytics, or technical maintenance.
  • Legal Purposes: Disclosure of information when required by law or authorities.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, please note that no method of data transmission or electronic storage is completely secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data.
  • Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data under certain conditions.
  • Objection: Object to the processing of your data based on specific circumstances.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to analyze website usage and tailor consulting content to user interests. All data collected for analytical purposes is anonymized and used exclusively to improve our consulting services.

7. Links to External Websites

Our website may include links to external sites. We have no control over their privacy practices and encourage you to review their individual policies.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Updates to this Privacy Policy will be posted on this page. We recommend checking it periodically to stay informed about any changes.

9. Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy or our data handling practices, please contact us through our contact page.

Note: All content on this website is intended solely for consulting and educational purposes. No purchase recommendations or promotional content for restricted financial instruments are provided.
